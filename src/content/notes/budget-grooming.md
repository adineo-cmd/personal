## Skincare Tips

Daily Habits:

- ✅ Wash face 2x daily (morning + night)
- ✅ Apply sunscreen before going out
- ✅ Moisturize after washing face
- ✅ Change pillowcase weekly
- ✅ Don't touch face with dirty hands
- ❌ Don't use hot water on face
- ❌ Don't skip sunscreen
- ❌ Don't pop pimples

## For Jawline Definition

- Chew gum regularly (jaw exercise)
- Reduce body fat (overall weight loss)
- Maintain good posture
- Tongue posture (mewing) - tongue on roof of mouth
- Stay hydrated (reduces water retention)

## Hair Care

Budget Routine:

- Shampoo 2-3x/week (not daily)
- Conditioner every wash
Oil massage once/week (coconut/olive)
- Trim every 6-8 weeks

Products:

- Shampoo: Head & Shoulders (₹120)
- Conditioner: Dove (₹100)
- Hair Oil: Parachute Coconut (₹80)

## Hygiene Checklist

Daily:

- □ Shower + soap full body
- □ Brush teeth 2x (2 mins each)
- □ Wash face 2x
- □ Apply deodorant
- □ Clean nails

Weekly:

- □ Trim nails (fingers + toes)
- □ Wash bedsheets
- □ Clean ear (gently)
- □ Exfoliate face (optional)

Monthly:

- □ Haircut
- □ Check skin for changes

## Acne Management

Prevention:

- Wash pillowcase weekly
- Don't share towels
- Keep phone screen clean
- Avoid oily foods

Treatment (Budget):

- Benzoyl Peroxide gel (₹150)
- Tea tree oil (₹100)
- Aloe vera gel (₹80)

If severe: Consult dermatologist
