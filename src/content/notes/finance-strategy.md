# Banking & Finance Strategy

Personal finance system for students and young professionals. Build wealth through disciplined saving and smart investing.

## Account Structure (4-Account System)

| Account | Purpose | Allocation |
|---------|---------|------------|
| **Salary** | Income received | 100% income |
| **Expense** | Daily spending | 50% of income |
| **Emergency** | 6 months expenses | 20% of income |
| **Investment** | Long-term wealth | 30% of income |

### Flow Diagram

Salary Account (₹50,000)

├── Expense Account (₹25,000) → Rent, Food, Bills

├── Emergency Account (₹10,000) → Until 6 months saved

└── Investment Account (₹15,000) → SIP, Stocks, Crypto

## 50/30/20 Budget Rule

| Category | Percentage | Example (₹50k) |
|----------|------------|----------------|
| **Needs** | 50% | ₹25,000 |
| **Wants** | 30% | ₹15,000 |
| **Savings** | 20% | ₹10,000 |

**Needs:** Rent, food, utilities, transport
**Wants:** Entertainment, dining out, hobbies
**Savings:** Emergency fund + investments

## Investment Vehicles

| Option | Risk | Returns | Lock-in |
|--------|------|---------|---------|
| **Index Funds** | Medium | 12-15% | None |
| **Mutual Funds (SIP)** | Medium | 12-15% | None |
| **PPF** | Low | 7-8% | 15 years |
| **Fixed Deposit** | Low | 6-7% | 1-5 years |
| **Crypto** | High | Variable | None |
| **Stocks** | High | 15-20% | None |

## Beginner Investment Plan

Month 1-6:

├── Build emergency fund (6 months expenses)

├── Start SIP ₹5000/month (Nifty 50 Index Fund)

└── Learn about investing (books, YouTube)
Month 7-12:

├── Increase SIP to ₹10,000/month

├── Add small crypto allocation (5% max)

└── Start learning stock analysis

Year 2+:

├── Diversify (Index + Stocks + PPF)

├── Increase SIP with income growth

└── Consider real estate (long-term)

## Crypto Strategy (High Risk)

- Allocation: Max 5-10% of portfolio

Recommended:

- Bitcoin (BTC): 50% of - crypto allocation
- Ethereum (ETH): 30% of crypto allocation
- Altcoins: 20% of crypto allocation

Rules:

- ✅ DCA (Dollar Cost Average)
- ✅ Use reputed exchanges (CoinDCX, WazirX)
- ✅ Store long-term in cold wallet
- ❌ No leverage/futures trading
- ❌ No FOMO buying
- ❌ Never invest emergency money

## Emergency Fund

- Target: 6 months of expenses
- Example: ₹25,000/month expenses = ₹1,50,000 fund

Where to keep:

- High-yield savings account
- Liquid mutual funds
Fixed deposit (breakable)

When to use:

- ✅ Job loss
- ✅ Medical emergency
- ✅ Major repair
- ❌ Not for vacations, gadgets, investments

## Credit Card Rules

- ✅ Pay full bill every month (no interest)
- ✅ Use for rewards/cashback
- ✅ Keep utilization below 30%
- ✅ Build credit score (750+)
- ❌ Never carry balance
- ❌ Never withdraw cash
- ❌ Never miss payment
- ❌ Don't have more than 3 cards

## Net Worth Tracking

Monthly Check:

Assets:

- Cash/Savings
- Investments
- Property value

Liabilities:

- Loans
- Credit card debt
- Net Worth = Assets - Liabilities

Goal: Increase every month
