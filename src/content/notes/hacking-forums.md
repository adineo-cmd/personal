## Community Etiquette

✅ Do:

Read rules before posting
Search before asking questions
Share writeups after solving
Respect NDA agreements
Credit others' work

❌ Don't:

Ask for illegal services
Share active exploits publicly
Dox or harass members
Spam or self-promote excessively
Request help with illegal activities
