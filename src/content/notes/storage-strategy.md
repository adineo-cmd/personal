
# Contact & Storage Strategy

Organizing your digital life requires a clear strategy for how you handle communication and where you store data.

## Email Segmentation

Maintain separate email addresses for different purposes to keep your inbox clean:

1. **Personal:** For friends, family, and important accounts.
2. **Professional:** For work-related communication.
3. **Spam/Signups:** For newsletters, shopping, and temporary accounts.

## Device Usage Strategy

- **Desktop PC:** Handle large file processing, video editing, and heavy storage tasks.
- **Laptop:** Use for research, writing, coding, and portable work.
- **Cloud:** Sync essential documents across devices using services like Syncthing or encrypted cloud storage.

## Backup Routines

Follow the **3-2-1 Rule**:

- **3** copies of your data.
- **2** different media types (e.g., SSD + HDD).
- **1** copy offsite (e.g., Cloud or physical drive at a different location).
