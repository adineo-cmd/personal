# Free Learning Resources

You don't need to spend a fortune to educate yourself. Here is a curated list of high-quality free resources across various domains.

## Online Courses

- **Coursera:** Offers free audit tracks for courses from top universities.
- **edX:** Similar to Coursera, providing access to university-level courses.

## Books & Literature

- **Project Gutenberg:** A library of over 60,000 free eBooks, mostly public domain.
- **LibGen (Library Genesis):** A search engine for articles and books (use responsibly regarding copyright).

## Coding & Development

- **freeCodeCamp:** Interactive coding lessons and certifications completely free.

## Design & Assets

- **Canva:** Free graphic design tool with templates.
- **Unsplash:** High-resolution free stock photos.
