# Time Management Techniques

Effective time management is essential for maintaining productivity and reducing stress. Below are some of the most proven techniques and tools to help you manage your time better.

## 1. The Pomodoro Technique

Work in focused sprints of 25 minutes, followed by a 5-minute break. After four cycles, take a longer break (15–30 minutes). This helps maintain mental agility.

## 2. Eisenhower Matrix

Categorize tasks based on urgency and importance:

- **Urgent & Important:** Do immediately.
- **Important & Not Urgent:** Schedule for later.
- **Urgent & Not Important:** Delegate if possible.
- **Not Urgent & Not Important:** Delete or ignore.

## 3. Time Blocking

Divide your day into blocks of time. Each block is dedicated to accomplishing a specific task or group of tasks. This reduces context switching.

## 4. The 2-Minute Rule

If a task takes less than two minutes to complete, do it immediately rather than adding it to your to-do list.
